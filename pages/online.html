INTRUCTOR-FACILITATED ONLINE LEARNING

Ed2Go

Convenient & Affordable, Instructor led format, Accessible Online 24/7

Most Ed2Go noncredit online courses are now $110.00! Non-Maryland residents add $5.00. Most classes run for six weeks, and they start monthly. Enrolling and paying for an Ed2Go online course is a two step process.

  • Step One: Select and ENROLL in the course.
  • Step Two: REGISTER/PAY Montgomery College for the course.
  • Learn more about the Ed2Go classes and how to enroll and pay for your class.

ONLINE CERTIFICATES AND COURSES

UGOTCLASS

Online Certificates and Courses "Skills for the 21st Century"

UGotClass offers online courses that are interactive, with multimedia, and nationally recognized experts as your online teachers. Courses may be taken individually, and some course sequences are bundled into Certificates. Classes are usually four weeks long, and are repeated quarterly.
