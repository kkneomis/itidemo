Becoming an Instructor for ITI

WD&CE's Information Technology Institute has a wide variety of noncredit, hands-on computer training classes ranging from very basic computer skills to advanced computer certification classes. Classes are offered in Microsoft Office Skills, Business Applications, Graphics and Publishing, Web Internet and Social Media, Programming Languages, Relational Databases, CISCO, Microsoft Networking Certification classes and more.See our Course Objectives for a listing of all of our ITI classes.
